About RM Resorts

RM Resorts are located just 20-minutes away from Male’s international airport by luxury catamaran. Among the resort’s 90 villas and suites are seven Infinity Pool Ocean Villas equipped with water slides, and private beach.

While the grown-ups are at the main spa or enjoying cocktails, kids can retreat to one of the largest children’s clubs in the Maldives.
